About the Club

Building Problem Solvers, Not Just Coders

Competitive programming is the sport of algorithms. You solve carefully designed problems with speed, logic, and clean implementation. At SGIPC, we focus on consistency, collaboration, and confidence under contest pressure.

What We Do

We organize structured practice tracks covering data structures, graph theory, dynamic programming, number theory, and contest strategy. Every member gets guided progression and clear milestones.

Our Mission

Create an inclusive space where students level up through practice, peer mentoring, and meaningful feedback. We celebrate small wins, learn from wrong submissions, and grow together.

Achievements

Results That Reflect Consistent Practice

Our members continue to push boundaries in local and international contests.

ICPC World Finalists 2024

A team from KUET reached the ICPC World Finals for the first time in 2024, marking a historic milestone for the university, setting a new benchmark for excellence within our club and inspiring future competitors.

Asia West Finalists 2026

Eight teams from KUET participated in the ICPC Dhaka Regional 2026. One team secured a top 20 position overall, became the Khulna divisional champion, and qualified for the ICPC Asia West Finals—marking another significant milestone for the club.

BUET IUPC 2026 — 4th Place

A team from KUET secured 4th place at the BUET Inter University Programming Contest (IUPC) 2026, demonstrating exceptional problem-solving skills and competitive excellence among top universities.
